# Inventory-Order-Recommendation-Engine
Using historical sales and current inventory levels, the system calculates average daily sales and classifies products into an 80/15/5 Pareto (ABC) distribution based on their share of total sales. Each class has a distinct ordering policy that determines replenishment quantities required to meet demand through the next delivery date. The system also identifies low-turnover items that may be candidates for markdown or removal.
